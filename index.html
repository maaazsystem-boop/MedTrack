MedPath
Version 3.3
The free MBBS study tracker built for Indian medical students. No fluff, no fees, no compromise.
Mission
Every Indian MBBS student deserves a structured, intelligent, and honest way to track their learning — regardless of which college they attend or how much money they have. MedPath exists to give that to every student, free, forever.
How It Works
1
Mark topics as you study
Track status and rate your confidence on each topic across all subjects.
2
AI builds your daily plan
The algorithm analyses your progress, confidence, and time gaps to recommend exactly what to study today.
3
Spaced repetition kicks in
Topics come back for revision at 3, 7, 14, and 30-day intervals — the scientifically proven retention method.
4
Track, analyse, improve
See exactly where you stand across every subject, tier, and topic. No surprises before exams.
Privacy
🔒
Everything — your progress, notes, streaks, confidence scores — lives only on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere. No account, no tracking, no ads. Ever.
Built By
MedPath was built by Maaaz, a 2nd Year MBBS student at ASMC Gonda, on an iPad, with zero budget and zero server. He got tired of scattered notes and built the tool he needed. If it helps you too — that's the whole point.
