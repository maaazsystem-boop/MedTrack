MedPath
Version 3.2 · Phase B
Your path through medicine, perfectly tracked
Our Mission
MedPath exists to give every Indian MBBS student — regardless of college or resources — a scientific, structured, and deeply personal way to track their learning journey. We believe organised study, powered by spaced repetition and smart prioritisation, is the difference between passing and excelling.
How It Works
1
Mark topics as you study
Track status (Not Started → In Progress → Done) and rate your confidence on each topic.
2
AI builds your daily plan
Our algorithm analyses your progress, confidence scores, and time since last revision to recommend exactly what to study today.
3
Spaced repetition kicks in
Topics you've studied are automatically scheduled for revision at 3, 7, 14, and 30-day intervals — the scientifically proven way to retain information.
4
Track your streaks & progress
Your daily streak motivates consistency. Progress analytics show exactly where you stand and what needs attention.
Core Philosophy
  • 🔴Essential topics (high-yield, always in exams) get studied first — always
  • 🧠Low confidence + long gap = highest priority for revision
  • 📚NMC competency codes align every topic to the official curriculum
  • ⚡Consistency beats cramming — 5 topics daily beats 50 topics once a month
  • 🎯Your year is your context — 2nd year topics take priority if you're in 2nd year
Privacy
🔒
All your data — study progress, notes, streaks, confidence scores — is stored only on your device using browser localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. No account. No tracking. No ads. Ever.
Built By
MedPath was built by Maaaz, a 2nd Year MBBS student at ASMC Gonda, who got frustrated with scattered notes, forgotten topics, and no real way to track what he'd studied. So he built the tool he wished existed. Built entirely on an iPad, with no server, no budget, and a lot of determination.
Tech Stack
  • 💻Pure HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies
  • ☁️Hosted on GitHub Pages — free, fast, reliable
  • 💾localStorage — 100% offline, 100% private
  • 📱Mobile-first design — built for phones and iPads
